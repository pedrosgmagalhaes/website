skinnyfeelings

skinnyfeelings was born in the middle of the Web3 revolution, and comes from a world of 10,000 unique collectible characters. While they are one of many, like other pop punk kids, they don't exactly fit in. They are a renegade among NFTs. They’re tech savvy, into skateboarding and definitely know that the world is larger than the blockchain - and music is their vehicle to escape. They dye their hair, wear glasses, and play their pixelated guitar. Their style is heavily influenced by other pop punk artists who came before them, but they’re not afraid to pave his own way.

Between getting their next tattoo and hanging out with their close circle of outsiders they find solace in recording new music. While skinnyfeelings takes inspiration from the past, our new pop punk champion enjoys putting their own twist on things. They’ve been reworking a few tracks and the first single is their very own version of the massive record “Heat Waves.”
