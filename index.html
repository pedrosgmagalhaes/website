What is Player Zero

Emerging from the virtual world, Player Zero introduces the AVA (Animated Virtual Artist) to entertain music fans in a new way. AVA’s enable limitless possibilities within the Player Zero Universe to champion songwriters and producers. Fans now have the opportunity to participate in the creative direction and success of the AVA’s. Creativity should have no limits.
